What Is Design Thinking?

Design thinking is a human-centered approach to innovation that emphasizes understanding the needs and experiences of users. It combines empathy, creativity, and rationality to solve complex problems, focusing on generating ideas and testing solutions iteratively.

Design thinking is increasingly adopted across various industries, from technology to healthcare. In recent surveys, many professionals highlighted its effectiveness in fostering collaboration and driving creative solutions. It encourages teams to think outside the box and embrace experimentation, making it a valuable tool for modern problem-solving.

Its emphasis on user involvement and iterative feedback has led to a growing community of practitioners who share insights and resources.
